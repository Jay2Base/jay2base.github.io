Measuring the true value of feedback

We believe the best measure of your performance is what your colleagues see in you and that the value of feedback is more than just the words.

Data driven measurement of continuous feedback

We have two key principals:

1. The traditional way of performance management via feedback is broken. giving and recieving feedback should be an intrinsic and regular part of an organisation, not restricted to a few carefully chosen individuals at an arbritarary time of year

2. Feedback should be measured on it all its merits not just what is said. Who, when and why feedback is given tells you as nuch if not more than the what is said. Everything about every piece of feedback contributes to itsvalue and should be measured.
